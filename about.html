About Me

Background


Hello! My name is Sungmin Lee and I'll be a Sophomore next year. I have a older brother named Josh who I love to talk to and two amazing parents. Some hobbies I have are programming (as seen with this website), playing the piano, doing Taekwondo, and reading books. I look forward to meeting new people and making friends!

Experience


I have a great passion for leadership, this year alone I was the Freshmen Class President and helped lead the class in events, fundraisers, and more. I achieved my 3rd degree black-belt which trained my endurance, discipline, and leadership. I helped lower-belts of various ages to train and achieve their own black-belts, through that I learned to work with anyone and to keep a positive mindset! Also, I reached advanced level for Piano CM (Certificate of Merit) and had completed over 100 volunteer hours and received the Gold Presidential Award. I was a part of my middle school's leadership class in 8th grade and tried out to be drum major/wood wind captain for band. I'm in various clubs at Branham and applied to become CSF's Secretary and Robotics club President. Even though I'm just starting out as secretary for CSF I'm committed and ready to do my best. Through all of this I've learned invaluable skills in leadership, organization, responsibility, and my personality. I hope to use all the skills I've gained to help Branham become the best it can.
